Sous Vide Chicken With Truffle Veloute

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, skin on
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp truffle butter
  • 1 bay leaf, thyme sprigs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Baby vegetables (turnips, asparagus, onions)

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken, add truffle butter and herbs, vacuum seal.
  2. Sous vide at 63°C (145°F) for 1 hour.
  3. Sear skin-side down until crisp.
  4. Make velouté: melt butter, whisk in flour, add stock, simmer until thick.
  5. Plate chicken with velouté and vegetables.
