Duck Breast With Cherry Gastrique

Ingredients:

  • 2 duck breasts
  • 1 cup cherries, pitted
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
  • ½ cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. Score duck skin, season, and sear skin-side down until crisp.
  2. Flip and cook to medium-rare (57°C / 135°F).
  3. Caramelize sugar, add vinegar, reduce.
  4. Add cherries and stock, simmer until thick.
  5. Finish with butter, serve over sliced duck.
