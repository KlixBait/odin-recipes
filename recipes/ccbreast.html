Herb-Crusted Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, skin on
  • 1 cup fresh breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped thyme
  • 1 tbsp chopped rosemary
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Mix breadcrumbs, herbs, garlic, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush chicken with Dijon mustard, then press herb mixture onto the skin side.
  4. Sear skin-side down in olive oil until golden.
  5. Transfer to oven and bake for 15–20 minutes until cooked through.

    6. Wallah you now have one of the best dishes around the world!
